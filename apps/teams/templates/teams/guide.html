User Guide & Documentation

Instructions for managing project teams, assignments, and submissions.

Student Instructions

1. Creating or Joining a Team

After logging in, use the registration portal to either join an existing team or create a new one. The first member to register for a new team is designated as the **Team Leader**.

2. Defining Roles (Real-Time)

On your dashboard, use the "Edit Role" button to specify your primary responsibility. Thanks to our new AJAX engine, your role is updated **instantly** without refreshing the page.

3. Submitting Assignments

Active assignments are listed on your dashboard. Upload your project files (PDF or ZIP) before the stated deadline.
Note: New uploads automatically replace all previous files for that assignment.

4. Viewing Grades

Once the lecturer has graded your submission and released the results, your scores and feedback will appear on the assignment cards.

Lecturer Instructions

1. Managing Class Materials

Upload course documents, reference materials, and project guides to the "Class Materials" section for all students to access.

2. Creating Assignments

Create new assignments by providing a title, description, and deadline. You can use the Active/Locked toggle to close submissions manually at any time.

3. Live Grading

The "Assignment Submissions" table provides a live view of all team uploads. Use the inline "Grade" button to assign scores and feedback instantly—no page reloads required.

4. Releasing Results

Grades remain private until you click "Release All." Use this button only when you have completed evaluations for the entire class.

Developer Command Center

High-density NOC-style interface for system telemetry (DB Latency, System Load) and incident log sanitization. Features an integrated Cloud Command Center for internal storage analytics, providing deep visibility into Cloudinary usage and media integrity without leaving the platform.

Staff Access & Verification

New Lecturer and Developer accounts are initially set to **Pending**. An administrator will review your application and grant access within 24 hours.
