Instructional Protocol

User Guide & Handbook

Everything you need to navigate the DSP Class Registration & Assignment platform with precision.

Student Protocol

01. Team Initialization

After login, visit the Portal to join an existing unit or initialize a new one. The first agent to register becomes the **Unit Leader**.

02. Role Designation

Use the "Set Role" tool on your Hub. Define whether you are a Lead Developer, Analyst, or Researcher to ensure the Unit is balanced.

03. Mission Submission

Active missions are displayed in your Task Pipeline. Upload PDFs or Source Packages before the tactical deadline. Late uploads are flagged automatically.

04. Debriefing

Check the "Task Pipeline" cards for evaluation scores and lecturer debriefs (feedback) once results are broadcasted.

Lecturer Protocol

01. Resource Deployment

Upload lab materials and reference guides to the **Field Repository**. These are instantly accessible to all active units.

02. Mission Briefing

Initialize new assignments with a title, description, and hard deadline. Attach briefing PDFs to guide the student units.

03. Unit Evaluation

Access the "Submission Tracking" board to review packages. Allocate scores and provide tactical feedback to each team individually.

04. Result Broadcast

Scores are private by default. Click **"Release All"** once evaluation is complete to sync the results to student dashboards.

Technical Signal Interference?

Contact the department administrator or audit the Project Blueprint for system details.
