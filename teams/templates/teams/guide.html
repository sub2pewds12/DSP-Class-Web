User Guide & Handbook

A quick guide to navigating the DSP Class Registration & Assignment platform.

Student Instructions

1. Joining a Team

After signing up, you must either create a new named team or join an existing one from the dropdown list. Teams have a maximum size set by the system.

2. Leadership & Project Info

The first person to join a team is the Leader. Leaders can update the project name and objectives. All members can (and should) set their specific Roles in the team.

3. Submitting Work

Active assignments will appear on your dashboard. Download the instruction files if provided, and upload your team's work before the deadline. Late submissions are allowed but will be flagged.

4. Viewing Results

Numeric scores and detailed feedback from the lecturer will appear on your assignment card once the grades have been officially released.

Lecturer Instructions

1. Resource Repository

Upload lecture slides, templates, or helpful documents to the "Class Materials" section. Students can view and download these at any time.

2. Assignment Management

Create new tasks by setting a title, description, and Deadline. You can optionally attach an instruction PDF for each task.

3. Grading Flow

Use the tracking table to see who has submitted. Click "Grade" to enter a numeric score (0-100) and specific feedback for a team's work.

4. Releasing Grades

Grades and feedback are private by default. When you are finished grading the class, click "Release Grades" on the assignment card to make them visible to students.

Need Technical Help?

Contact the department administrator or check the project Source Code for more details.
